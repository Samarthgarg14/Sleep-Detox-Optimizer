🌙 What is Sleep Detox Optimizer?

Sleep Detox Optimizer is your smart assistant designed to monitor and enhance your nightly recovery. It analyzes hydration, stress, screen time, and routine to generate personalized detox scores and tips.

🧠 The Science Behind Sleep Detox

During deep sleep, the brain’s glymphatic system clears out metabolic waste, reducing the risk of cognitive decline. Similarly, liver detox processes are most active during early night hours, and heart rate variability reflects recovery quality.

⚙️ How It Works

  • 🧪 Tracks your hydration, dinner time, screen exposure & stress levels
  • 📊 Calculates detox impact on Brain, Heart, and Liver systems
  • 🤖 AI chatbot offers tips to boost your sleep detox effectiveness

🛌 Steps to Maximize Detox

  1. 💧 Drink enough water (at least 6-8 glasses daily)
  2. 🍽️ Finish your dinner 2 hours before sleep
  3. 📵 Reduce screen time 1 hour before bed
  4. 🧘 Practice breathing exercises to lower stress
  5. 🛏️ Aim for 7-8 hours of deep, restful sleep

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How is Detox Score calculated?

Detox Score is based on your hydration, dinner timing, screen usage, stress level, and wearable health data like sleep, HRV, and SpO₂.

Q2: Is this medically certified?

No, this is a wellness tracking tool. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice.

Q3: Can I improve my detox scores?

Absolutely! Small daily changes like earlier dinner, more water, and less screen time can boost your scores.
